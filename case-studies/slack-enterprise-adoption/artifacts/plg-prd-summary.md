# PLG PRD Summary - Slack Enterprise Motion

## Product Goal
Turn individual discovery into enterprise-wide paid adoption with measurable productivity gains.

## North Star
Messages sent per organization per week.

## Input Metrics
- 14-day activation rate (2,000 messages milestone).
- DAU/MAU engagement ratio.
- Free-to-paid conversion.

## Output Metrics
- Net revenue retention (NRR).
- Expansion revenue.
- 3-year enterprise ROI.

## Key Requirements
- Time-to-value under 5 minutes.
- Searchable persistent channels.
- Integrations and automation.
- Enterprise admin, security, and compliance controls.
