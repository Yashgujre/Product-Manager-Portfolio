# Enterprise Rollout Runbook (IBM-Style)

## Phase 1: Grassroots Adoption (Months 1-6)
- Seed high-collaboration teams.
- Track usage and integration depth.

## Phase 2: Team Expansion (Months 6-12)
- Expand to adjacent functions.
- Standardize channel naming and governance.

## Phase 3: IT and Compliance Review (Months 12-18)
- Validate SSO/SAML, audit, DLP, and data residency.
- Finalize migration and retention controls.

## Phase 4: Enterprise Contract and Scale
- Execute Enterprise Grid contract.
- Deploy centralized admin controls and onboarding enablement.

## Exit Criteria
- Security and compliance sign-off complete.
- Executive KPI dashboard operational.
- Adoption thresholds met across key business units.
