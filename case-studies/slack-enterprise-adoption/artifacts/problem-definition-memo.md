# Problem Definition Memo - Slack Enterprise PLG

## Objective
Define the communication and workflow problem that Slack solves in enterprise environments.

## Core Problem Statement
Enterprise teams lose speed and context because communication is fragmented across email, chat, and disconnected tools.

## Evidence Snapshot
- 80%+ enterprise messaging share held by Outlook/Exchange at launch window.
- Workers spend 28% of work time in email and 20% searching for information.
- Teams use ~5.2 internal communication tools, creating context fragmentation.

## User Segments
- Individual contributors in cross-functional teams.
- Engineering, product, and operations managers.
- Enterprise IT/security leaders responsible for governance.

## Success Criteria
- Faster team activation and habit formation.
- Lower communication overhead.
- Better retrieval of institutional knowledge.
